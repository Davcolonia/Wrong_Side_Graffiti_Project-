Summary

The purpose of this project is to bring insight and data to the graffiti culture. This project uses beautiful soup, tableau, html, and sklearn to visualize my senior thesis

A large part of the project is dedicated to story telling and playing anthropologist. Much of the categorical info came via personal knowledge; images and stats from online.
