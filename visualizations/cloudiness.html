Dream TDK

Dream was a muralist in the SF area. Despite passing more than 15 years ago multiple tribute peices exist in his honor

Tie

Tie was one of the most prolific writers in Sf for most of the 90s. His tags continue to be seen to this day despite being active almost 30 years ago. His life was cut short via homocide after trespassing on an SF rooftop.

ORFN

ORFN was a prolific writer in the SF scene since the 90s. ORFN has the designation of continuing to be up untill his passing in 2016. His style is noted for its originality and minimalist charm.

Reminisce

Reminisce is one of the more notable integral to the Mission School that sprung from 90s art in SF. Along with Barry Mcgee Reminisce can be considered a trail blazer in bringing artistic legitimacy to graffiti.

Twist/Barry McGee

Barry McGee is one of the most prolific writers in the modern era for essentially transitioning from gritty street vandal to a legitimate artistic figure.

Giraffa

Giraffa is a prolific writer from the 2000s whose work spanned most of the West Coast. At one point it was a gag that you couldnt go to a spot or freeway that didn't have at least one giraffa work.
